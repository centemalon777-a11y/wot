Physics Note: Machines

1. What is a Machine?

A machine is a device that makes work easier by multiplying force, changing the direction of force, or increasing the distance over which the force is applied.

2. Types of Simple Machines

  • Lever
  • Inclined Plane
  • Wheel and Axle
  • Pulley
  • Screw
  • Wedge

3. Mechanical Advantage (MA)

Mechanical Advantage is the ratio of the output force to the input force.

Formula: MA = Load / Effort

4. Velocity Ratio (VR)

Velocity Ratio is the ratio of the distance moved by the effort to the distance moved by the load.

Formula: VR = Distance moved by Effort / Distance moved by Load

5. Efficiency (η)

Efficiency is the ratio of useful work output to the work input expressed in percentage.

Formula: η = (MA / VR) × 100%

6. Uses of Machines

  • To lift heavy loads
  • To reduce effort
  • To change the direction of force
  • To increase speed or distance
