Physics Note: Scalars and Vectors

1. Scalars

Scalars are physical quantities that have magnitude only. They do not have direction.

Examples of Scalars:

  • Mass
  • Temperature
  • Speed
  • Distance
  • Time

2. Vectors

Vectors are physical quantities that have both magnitude and direction.

Examples of Vectors:

  • Displacement
  • Velocity
  • Acceleration
    •   
  • Force
